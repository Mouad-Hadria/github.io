E2D Analytical Solution

PROJECT OVERVIEW

The project focuses on building an end-to-end (E2E) analytic solution to track the sales performance of bike retail store. The solution is designed to scale, providing broader business insights and facilitating the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) capabilities to unlock the full potential of business growth. The solution was delivered using Microsoft Fabric to ensure it meets the business needs, including building a semantic model that serves as a data warehouse using the Lakehouse Medallion Architecture.

BUISNIESS ANALYSIS

  • Overview of Business Needs

    A bicycle retail store has experienced significant business growth, making traditional data reporting more challenging. The store decided to invest in an intelligent data solution that is easily accessible and facilitates collaboration between both technical and non-technical personnel. With the increasing interest in artificial intelligence (AI) due to its substantial benefits, the store aims to implement a solution that can seamlessly integrate AI capabilities.

    The store plans to transition gradually, starting with essential tools to track sales performance. Once established, they will scale the solution to cover more business needs, powered by AI capabilities.

  • Approach

    After a comprehensive evaluation of the bicycle retail store's business needs, we chose to build the project with Microsoft Fabric. This decision is based on Fabric's capacity to deliver a complete, integrated analytics environment, which facilitates collaboration between data professionals and business users. Fabric’s platform encompasses all stages of the data lifecycle—ingestion, storage, processing, and analysis—within a single, cohesive environment.

    For this project, Microsoft Fabric offers a robust foundation and scalability. It supports both citizen and professional data practitioners, integrating various tools essential for business decision-making. The platform includes a range of services such as data engineering, data integration, data warehousing, real-time intelligence, data science, and business intelligence. By utilizing Fabric, we streamline our operations by avoiding the need for multiple services from different vendors. Fabric's single, user-friendly product simplifies setup, management, and usage, with optimized experiences and tools tailored to different user roles.

    Additionally, Microsoft Fabric provides a unified Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) solution with all data stored in an open format within OneLake. This central repository is accessible to all analytics engines on the platform, offering scalability, cost-effectiveness, and remote accessibility.
