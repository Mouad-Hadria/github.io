CHARGING AHEAD: ANALYZING ELECTRIC VEHICLE TITLE AND REGISTRATION ACTIVITIES THROUGH WASHINGTON STATE OF LICENSING

In this data analysis project, we have meticulously explored the extensive datasets provided by the Washington State Licensing Department. By harnessing both transaction counts and electric vehicle data, our objective is to unveil valuable insights. Our analytical approach has enabled us to effectively identify leading brands, models, and electric vehicle categories, differentiating between Battery Electric Vehicles (BEV) and Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEV).

SKYWARD BOUND: ANALYZING DELTA AIR LINES' PRE AND POST-MERGER PERFORMANCE WITH NORTHWEST AIRLINES

The objective of this data analysis project is to evaluate the performance of Delta Air Lines both pre- and post-merger with Northwest Airlines. The project aims to develop a comprehensive dashboard that will concentrate on analyzing crucial metrics and key performance indicators (KPIs) within the aviation industry. These metrics include revenue, profitability, airline traffic, employee compensation, and productivity.

Data Analysis of San Francisco International Airport

We embarked on a journey to explore historical passenger volume trends at San Francisco International Airport across various years. We conducted a comprehensive analysis to distinguish between the proportions of domestic and international travelers while identifying the primary airlines operating at the airport. Furthermore, we delved into the intricate network of collaborations established within the airport's interconnected environment.
