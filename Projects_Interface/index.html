DATA ANALYSIS OF SAN FRANCISCO INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT


We embarked on an exploration of the historical progression of passenger numbers at San Francisco International Airport throughout various years. Furthermore, an in-depth analysis was conducted to discern the proportions of domestic and international passengers, while also pinpointing the leading airlines in operation at the airport. Additionally, we delved into the intricate web of partnerships forged within the airport's ecosystem

SKYWARD BOUND: ANALYZING DELTA AIR LINES' PRE AND POST-MERGER PERFORMANCE WITH NORTHWEST AIRLINES


The goal of this data analyst project is to assess Delta Air Lines' performance before and after its merger with Northwest Airlines. It involves creating a dashboard to analyze important aviation industry metrics like revenue, profitability, airline traffic, employee compensation, and productivity. By using a data-driven approach, the project aims to gain insights into the merger's impact on Delta Air Lines' performance and understand the dynamics within the aviation industry.

ELECTRIFYING INSIGHTS: ANALYZING REGISTERED ELECTRIC VEHICLES IN WASHINGTON STATE DEPARTMENT OF LICENSING


In the course of this data analysis endeavor, we have painstakingly delved into the vast datasets generously offered by the Washington State Licensing Department. By leveraging both transaction records and electric vehicle information, our primary aim is to unearth valuable insights. Through our analytical methodology, we've successfully pinpointed the prominent brands, models, and electric vehicle classifications, making a clear distinction between Battery Electric Vehicles (BEV) and Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEV)
